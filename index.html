What is Kumpir?

Istanbul's streets,
Bombay's soul.

Kumpir is Turkey's legendary loaded baked potato — fluffy inside, crispy outside, mashed with butter and cheese, then piled with toppings you pick yourself.

Bluero brings this to Mumbai without compromise. Same recipe. Same soul. A new home.
